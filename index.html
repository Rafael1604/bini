THE NATIONS GIRL GROUP BINI

JHOANNA COLET MIKHA GWEN STACEY SHEENA AIAH MALOI

BINI is an eight-member P-Pop group from ABS-CBN's Star Magic that debuted in June 2021.
BINI draws inspiration from classic pop and hip-hop vibes and even sprinkles in some traditional Filipino sounds.
Think fun beats that make you want to dance, mixed with heartfelt messages that stick with you long after the song is over.

Maraiah Queen Arceta

Aiah is the group’s eldest member.
She’s the visual, main rapper, and sub-vocalist.
She’s known for her soft and dreamy voice and bright personality.
Fans who’ve met her agree; they all say she’s very approachable.

Nicolette Vergara

Colet is the group's main vocalist, lead dancer, and lead rapper.
She brings fierce energy and sharp dance moves to the stage.
Fans think Colet has a strong personality on-stage,
but is actually maalaga towards the members.
She’s also known as one of the tidiest members.

Mary Loi Yves Ricalde

Maloi is the third eldest member and one of the main vocalists.
Her passion for singing blossomed early—she started singing when she was three years old!
According to fans, Maloi is very friendly and easy to talk to.

Gweneth L. Apuli

Gwen is the lead vocalist and lead rapper.
She’s known for having a rich, deep voice as well as a sharp ear for music.
Fans say Gwen is shy and mahinhin when you meet her but very warm-hearted.

Stacey Aubrey Sevilleja

Stacey is the main rapper, lead dancer, and sub-vocalist.
Despite being one of the younger members,
Stacey is known for her maturity and dedicated work ethic.
Fans say she’s very kind, down-to-earth, and friendly.

Mikhaela Janna Lim

Mikha is the third youngest member, visual, main rapper, and lead dancer of the group.
She’s known for her video game skills!
Members have mentioned that she’s surprisingly good at them.
Fans view Mikha as kalog and an inner softie.

Jhoanna Christine Robles

Jhoanna is the group’s second youngest member and leader!
She’s also the lead vocalist and rapper.
She’s known to guide the group with warmth and affection.
Fans who’ve met her say she’s approachable, down-to-earth, and jolly.

Sheena Mae Catacutan

Sheena is the bunso of the group, the main dancer, and the sub-vocalist.
She’s always loved dancing and brings her vivacious energy to all her performances.
She's considered professional and playful, even photobombing fan pictures taken with other members.
